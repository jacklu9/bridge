What types of news content will be available?

来源：help.bbc.com · 快照时间 2026-07-17 03:54 UTC

On the BBC.com homepage, you'll find a mix of the biggest news stories of the moment, plus our editors' picks of timely, relevant and interesting stories and videos from culture, technology, history, business, and more.

  • To dig deeper into BBC News from around the globe, navigate to the News section of the website where you'll find the trusted BBC journalism you've come to rely on. This section will look a little different, but we haven't changed the articles, videos, and live coverage that's always found there.
  • We've added new sections to the site that you'll now find in the top navigation bar. Read the BBC's arts and entertainment coverage in the Culture section, technology, health, and science in Innovation, and all things sustainability in Earth.
  • Sport, Business, and Travel are still there, bringing you agenda-setting stories from around the world, as well as the delightful hidden gem stories readers have come to love from the BBC.
  • You'll see two new format sections as well: Video, which is where you can watch news videos and multimedia storytelling you'd recognize from the formerly-named Reel section; and Live, a space to follow a timeline of all live coverage that's currently unfolding across News and Sport on BBC.com.
  • Discover our newsletters, including The News Briefing, Tech Decoded, Future Earth, the brand-new In History, and The Essential List.
